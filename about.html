ABOUT PATLA KHAWA HIGH SCHOOL

OUR LEGACY

Located in the serene landscapes of Patla Khawa, Khagribari, Cooch Behar, West Bengal (PIN: 736165), Patla Khawa High School stands as a beacon of academic excellence. Since our inception, we have been dedicated to providing a holistic educational experience that balances rigorous intellectual training with character building and moral growth.

Our institution is more than just a school; it is a community of learners, innovators, and future leaders. We take pride in our rich tradition of academic success and our commitment to preparing students for the challenges of a rapidly changing world.
